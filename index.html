About Me

Welcome to my tactical hub! I'm Kiran Rasarit, a passionate gamer with a deep interest in military simulation (milsim) games and strategy-driven combat. With over 300 hours in Arma 3 and experience across various other milsim titles, I enjoy analyzing tactics, refining strategies, and immersing myself in realistic warfare simulations.

This website is a place where I share my insights, experiences, and tactical knowledge with like-minded players. Whether you're a beginner looking to get started or a seasoned tactician seeking to fine-tune your skills, you'll find valuable content here, including:

  • Discussions on realism, teamwork, and strategy
  • Tips for improving coordination and combat effectiveness

Feel free to explore, connect, and share your thoughts—let’s build a strong community of tacticians!
